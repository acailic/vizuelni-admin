## 1. UNDP Serbia - Digital Transformation

### Quick Reference

**Program:** Digital Transformation of Public Services  
**Amount:** $150,000  
**Duration:** 18 months  
**Deadline:** Rolling (submit by April 30, 2026 for priority)

### Executive Summary (250 words)

Vizuelni Admin Srbije (Визуелни Административни Подаци Србије) is an open-source data visualization platform designed specifically for Serbian government data. The platform addresses a critical gap: while Serbia has made significant progress in publishing open data through data.gov.rs, citizens and journalists lack accessible tools to visualize and understand this data.

**Our solution provides:**

1. Pre-built integrations with data.gov.rs (1,200+ datasets)
2. Serbian language support (Cyrillic and Latin scripts)
3. Geographic visualizations for all 174 Serbian municipalities
4. No-code chart creation for non-technical users
5. Export capabilities for publications and reports

**We request $150,000 to:**

- Expand to 10 pilot government agencies (5 confirmed)
- Train 200 civil servants in data visualization
- Create 50 public dashboards on government performance
- Develop comprehensive Serbian-language training materials
- Establish a sustainable open-source community

**Expected outcomes:**

- 50% increase in data.gov.rs dataset utilization
- 200+ government staff trained in data literacy
- 50 public-facing transparency dashboards
- 10x increase in data-driven journalism stories

This aligns with UNDP's Digital Transformation goals and Serbia's Public Administration Reform Strategy 2021-2030.

---

### Problem Statement

**The Challenge:**

Serbia has made commendable progress in open data publication through data.gov.rs, hosting 1,247 datasets from 47 government organizations. However, a significant gap exists between data availability and data utilization.

**Current Situation:**

- 1,247 datasets published on data.gov.rs
- <5% utilized in public discourse or journalism
- No Serbian-language visualization tools available
- Journalists rely on Excel or expensive foreign tools
- Government staff lack training in data communication

**Root Causes:**

1. **Technical Barrier:** Existing tools require programming knowledge
2. **Language Barrier:** No tools support Serbian Cyrillic properly
3. **Data Barrier:** Geographic data not pre-configured
4. **Knowledge Barrier:** Limited training in data visualization

**Evidence:**

- Survey of 50 Serbian journalists: 78% struggle with data visualization
- Only 2 of 25 government agencies use visualization tools
- data.gov.rs analytics show high downloads but low reuse
